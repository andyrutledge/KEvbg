Our Logos

Kalkomey Logos

The Kalkomey logo is always shown like this; with the logomark and logotype. Specifics on color and size use, as well as inappropriate use, follows below.

KE Logo Minimum Spatial Standards

The height and width yardstick for minimum spatial standards comes from the Kalkomey "basketball." No other textual, architectural, or graphic element may intrude upon this space surrounding the KE logo.

KE Logo Color Guidelines

The Kalkomey logo should typically be 85% black (#4b4b4d) logotype with the gradient ball. If the logo will be used at a very small size, the solid red ball version should be used. For black & white media, the solid black version is appropriate and for monochrome reversed contexts, the solid white version is appropriate.

If a color version of the logo is appropriate on a dark background, the logotype may adopt a medium gray (#aaa) color, keeping the gradient ball.

KE Logo Mistakes

Do not use nonstandard variations of the logo configuration or colors:

  • Do not use black or other nonstandard colors for the logotype in combination with the gradient ball
  • do not use a drop shadow or glow on any portion of the logo
  • Do not use nonstandard monocolor versions of the logo
  • do not use black or nonstandard monocolor ball with the gray logotype
  • dp not apply a stroke or outline to the logo
  • Do not use nonstandard reverse monocolor logos
  • Do not enclose the logo
